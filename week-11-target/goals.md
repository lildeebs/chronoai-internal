## Priority 1: Community — Identify & Map (Goal 1)

**Goal 1: Deliver 1 high-intent community map for the manifestation realm**

Identify and document 10+ communities (Reddit, Discord, Facebook groups, etc.) where SoulGarden’s target users already congregate — manifestation, law of attraction, mindfulness, spiritual wellness. Rank by intent signals, size, and fit. Use Week 10 signals (e.g. 10+ Android requests, 50 Reddit engagements, 58 KOL contacts) and colleague community findings to inform the map.

- **Deliverable:** 1 community map doc (Google Doc or sheet) with: list of 10+ communities, ranking criteria, and channel-community mapping. Optional: 3+ user segments (demographics, motivations, where they gather).
- **Quantitative:** 10+ high-intent communities identified and ranked; internal vs. organic installs separated in data where possible.
- **Core metric:** Pipeline quality — targeting the right communities accelerates progress to 200 installs and reduces wasted outreach. Direct strategic value: business direction is “community-first”; this deliverable defines *where* to show up.

---

## Priority 2: Community — Build & Drive (Goal 2)

**Goal 2: Deliver 1 community engagement plan and execute in 5+ high-intent communities**

Actively build presence in at least 5 communities from the map: add value (posts, replies, helpful content), and drive SoulGarden awareness and installs from community-sourced traffic. Track which communities drive sign-ups or installs.

- **Deliverable:** 1 short engagement plan (which communities, what actions, how to attribute) + evidence of execution (screenshots of posts/DMs, links, or tracking). 1 line in tracking: community-sourced installs (even if 0 this week — baseline matters).
- **Quantitative:** 5+ communities actively engaged; community-sourced installs recorded (target: contribute toward 200).
- **Core metric:** Community → install conversion. This is how the role adds value: building and driving the intended audience, not just “doing social.” Direct contribution to Gate 1.

## Priority 3: Nurture & Retain (Goal 3)

**Goal 3: Deliver 1 nurture plan and execute first touchpoints for existing SoulGarden users**

Convert existing installs (29 baseline) into active, retained users. Reach 100% of identifiable organic users; collect qualitative feedback from at least 5 real users; establish a Day-7 retention baseline.

- **Deliverable:** 1 nurture plan doc + evidence of execution (DMs, community posts, feedback requests). 1 short user feedback summary (qualitative insights from 5+ users).
- **Quantitative:** All identifiable organic users (est. 13–16) reached; 5+ feedback responses; 1 retention baseline (e.g. Day-7 active).
- **Core metric:** Retention and activation — Gate 1 is 200 *active* users. A retained user is worth more than 10 cold installs; this validates product–community fit before scaling.

## Priority 4: Content & Measurement (Goal 4)

**Goal 4: Publish all queued SoulGarden content and deliver 1 first-week performance report**

Deploy the 18 carousels & reels across owned + AI-powered TikTok accounts. Define what “working” looks like using real engagement data. Report impressions, reach, engagement rate, and top-performing content per channel.

- **Deliverable:** All 18 content pieces published. 1 performance report (sheet or doc) with metrics per channel; Upfluence/Agorapulse or equivalent configured and reporting.
- **Quantitative:** 18 posts published; min. 3% engagement rate target on TikTok; top-performing content and channels identified.
- **Core metric:** Content-to-community conversion — validates which content and channels drive community growth and installs. Feeds Week 12 strategy and supports 200 installs via scalable organic acquisition.

---

## Priority 5: Source of Truth (Goal 5)

**Goal 5: Deliver 1 working dashboard and 1 tracking framework for Gate 1 progress**

Establish the single source of truth for 200 installs: total installs, active users, retention, engagement, organic vs. internal breakdown, and (where possible) channel/community attribution. Prefer n8n automated dashboard with Calvin; otherwise a clear doc + sheet that updates at least daily.

- **Deliverable:** 1 live dashboard (link or screenshot) + 1 short tracking framework doc (metrics, data sources, update frequency).
- **Quantitative:** 6 core metrics configured and reporting: total installs, active users, retention rate, engagement rate, organic vs. internal split, channel/community attribution. Dashboard updated at least daily.
- **Core metric:** Data integrity — one place to check progress to 200 installs. Enables data-driven decisions for community and content; prevents false quantification in future goal-setting.
