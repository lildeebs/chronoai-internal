| **People Don't Want Tools — They Want Belonging** | PRCA insight | PRCA proved it — the interest wasn't just about buying a product. People want **to belong to something**. |

## Summary for Goal-Setting

- **PRCA learning:** Interest wasn't just product — people want **belonging**. Outreach and positioning should speak to that.
- **SoulGarden community** = users with daily rituals, shared habits, who **share and invite others**. Identify and nurture these first.
- **Outcome focus:** Retention through belonging; organic growth through advocates. Week 11 goals should measure belonging/retention and invite behaviour, not only contact count.
