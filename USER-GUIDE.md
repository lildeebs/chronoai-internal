# 员工使用说明 / USER GUIDE

**Weekly Goal Audit & Friday Review — CHO 审计与复盘闭环**

This document is the canonical reference for **reporting (Friday) decks** and **target (Monday) decks**. Use it when building or improving weekly slides and tasks.

---

## Purpose

- **Audience:** Employees using this workflow.
- **Goal:** Use the AI bot to complete the loop from **Monday goal-setting** to **Friday review**, for high-quality energy management.
- **Spirit:** The system gives **organizational empowerment and actionable advice**, not surveillance.

---

## I. Dual-Track Loop

The process has two tracks. The system routes your message by **trigger words**.

### Track 1: Early-Week Goal Audit (Monday / Target deck)

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **When** | Sunday evening through Monday. |
| **How** | Send text to the **Weekly Goal Audit Bot**. List goals line by line. |
| **Trigger** | Do **NOT** include “本周总结” or “Weekly Review” in this message, or the bot will treat it as a Friday review. |

**Example submission:**

```
1. Complete the launch of XX feature by Friday. Acceptance criteria: tests passed and deployed to production.
2. Publish the "YY Process Guidelines" document and sync it with the team.
```

**What you get:** A **[Goal Audit Report]** that:

- **Audit score & warning zone** — Overall score (out of 10) and main risks (e.g. no deliverables, no strategic alignment).
- **Item-by-item audit & revision guidance** — The bot does **not** rewrite goals for you. It points out missing elements and gives a **fill-in-the-blank framework** for you to complete.

**Your action:** Fill in deliverables and metrics, edit your goals, and send again until the system archives them.

---

### Track 2: Friday Review (Friday / Reporting deck)

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **When** | Friday. |
| **How** | Your message **MUST** contain **“本周总结”** (this week’s summary), preferably at the start, then your actual outputs and reflections. |
| **Trigger** | “本周总结” (or configured English trigger). |

**Example submission:**

```
本周总结：
1. Successfully launched XX feature, testing passed with 0 bugs.
2. YY process document is only half done due to blocked progress from the legal department.
3. Handled three sudden emergency online crash issues.
```

**What you get:** A **[Weekend Review Echo]** card that:

- Surfaces **hidden value** or extra work beyond your original goals.
- Acknowledges **organizational blockers** and difficulty.
- Offers **emotional support** and **cognitive reframing** suggestions.

**Deck rule:** **Next steps** from the previous week’s Friday deck become **this week’s goals/tasks** in the new week’s deck (and feed into Monday target audit).

## II. Audit Logic: What Is a “Good Goal”? (CHO Standard)

The system scores Monday goals against **5 rules**. More rules passed → higher score.

| Rule | Meaning | Good goal | Bad goal |
|------|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Binary verification** | On Friday you can clearly say “done” or “not done”. | Clear criteria: “Document published”, “Feature launched”. | “Push forward XX project”, “Make progress”. |
| **No “effort” verbs** | Don’t say “I’m working on it”; say what will be **delivered**. | “Complete”, “Produce”, “Publish”, “Deliver”. | “Follow up”, “Communicate”, “Research”, “Assist”. |
| **Outward value** | The goal is a **deliverable** (doc, code, data, link). | “XX Report”, data dashboard, feature shipped. | “Hold a meeting to discuss”, “Coordinate resources” (no deliverable). |
| **Strategic alignment** | It’s clear which **core business metric** this supports. | Tied to ARR, conversion, stability, cost. | Only describes activity, no business impact. |
| **Time matching** | Workload is about **60–80%** of core energy for the week. | 3–5 well-defined goals. | One goal for a month’s work; or many trivial tasks. |

## III. FAQ & Troubleshooting

- **Q: Bot says `RecordIdNotFound` or can’t find my record on Friday?**  
  **A:** You must have sent **pure goals** (no “本周总结”) to the bot on Sunday/Monday and received an audit. No goal record → no comparison. **Habit: pass Goal Audit first, then send Friday Review.**

- **Q: Can I send goals in English?**  
  **A:** Yes. The system supports bilingual detection and will return audit suggestions in English if you submit in English.

- **Q: My original text and the audit don’t match?**  
  **A:** Use a numbered list (“1. … 2. …”) or line breaks. Avoid one big unformatted block so the system can parse multiple items.

- **Q: I didn’t complete a goal because of external blockers. What do I do?**  
  **A:** In “本周总结”, write the blockers honestly (e.g. other departments, missing resources). The system is built to recognize “organizational friction” and will reflect that in feedback and visibility to management.

## Summary

- **Use the workflow well** = **Submit goals Sunday/Monday** (get coached and refined) + **Submit “本周总结” on Friday** (get systematic review feedback).
- **For decks:**  
  - **Monday (target):** Goals framed with CHO criteria; next steps from last week’s Friday deck become this week’s goals.  
  - **Friday (reporting):** This week’s outcomes and blockers; next steps stated clearly so they can become next week’s goals.
