# Week 11 targets (9 Mar – 13 Mar)

CHO-formatted goals: binary verification, clear deliverables, quantitative acceptance, strategic alignment. 4 goals.

CEO Direction: Week 11 focus is on **nurturing the existing community** and **identifying who the SoulGarden community actually is** — not just volume outreach.

Audit feedback applied: Tighten activity metrics into outcome metrics. Define conversion funnels. Show strategic leverage, not just contact counts.

---

## Goal 1: Identify the SoulGarden Community Profile

Deliver 1 SoulGarden community profile document by Friday that defines who our users are — demographics, motivations, behaviour patterns, and where they congregate online. Based on: (a) analysis of 29 existing app installs (separate internal vs. organic), (b) engagement patterns from Week 10's 50 Reddit community members and 58 KOL contacts, (c) 10+ Android request profiles, (d) colleague's Week 10 community findings (PRCA insights).

- **Deliverable**: 1 community profile doc (Google Doc or Lark doc) with user segments, personas, and channel-community mapping.
- **Quantitative**: 3+ distinct user segments identified, each with demographics + motivations + preferred channels. Internal vs. organic installs separated with data.
- **Core metric**: Community clarity — knowing exactly who to target accelerates conversion and reduces wasted outreach. Directly supports Gate 1 (200 active users) by focusing effort on highest-converting segments.

---

## Goal 2: Nurture Existing Community — Convert Installs to Active Users

Deliver 1 engagement plan and execute first nurture touchpoints for all existing SoulGarden users (29 installs) by Friday. Goal: convert passive installs into active, retained users. Track engagement via app analytics and direct outreach (DMs, community posts, feedback requests).

- **Deliverable**: 1 nurture plan doc + evidence of execution (screenshots of community interactions, DMs sent, feedback collected). 1 user feedback summary (qualitative insights from at least 5 real users).
- **Quantitative**: Reach out to 100% of identifiable organic users (est. 13–16). Collect qualitative feedback from at least 5 users. Measure Day-7 retention rate as baseline.
- **Core metric**: Retention and activation — a retained user is worth more than 10 new installs. Feeds into Gate 1 (200 *active* users, not just downloads). Validates product-community fit before scaling outreach.

---

## Goal 3: Deploy Content & Measure Performance

Publish all queued SoulGarden content (18 carousels & reels across owned accounts + 10 AI-powered TikTok accounts) and deliver first performance data review by Friday. Define what "working" looks like using real engagement data, not assumptions.

- **Deliverable**: All 18 content pieces published across platforms. 1 performance report (Google Sheet or doc) covering: impressions, reach, engagement rate, and top-performing content per channel. Upfluence + Agorapulse dashboards configured and reporting.
- **Quantitative**: 18 posts published. Min. 3% engagement rate target across TikTok accounts. First-week data review completed with actionable insights (what to double down on, what to stop).
- **Core metric**: Content-to-engagement conversion — validates which content types and channels drive community growth. Data feeds directly into Week 12 content strategy. Supports Gate 1 by identifying scalable organic acquisition channels.

---

## Goal 4: Build Source of Truth — Tracking & Measurement Infrastructure

Deliver 1 working dashboard (n8n automated with Calvin) and 1 detailed tracking framework by Friday. Establish the single source of truth for Gate 1 progress: total users, active users, retention, engagement, and organic vs. internal breakdown.

- **Deliverable**: 1 n8n dashboard (live link or screenshot of working state) + 1 tracking framework doc defining all metrics, data sources, and update frequency. User behaviour tracking live in app analytics.
- **Quantitative**: 6 core metrics configured and reporting (total installs, active users, retention rate, engagement rate, organic vs. internal split, channel attribution). Dashboard updated at least daily.
- **Core metric**: Data integrity — "Kill Illusions" by having one verifiable place to check Gate 1 progress. Enables data-driven decisions for Weeks 12+ and prevents false quantification in future goal-setting.
