# Slide 4 screen recording (WhatsApp chat)

The deck uses your screen recording from a **GitHub Release** so the file can be >100 MB.

## One-time setup

1. Open **Releases**: https://github.com/lildeebs/chronoai-internal/releases
2. Click **Draft a new release**
3. **Tag:** `week11-assets` (choose "Create new tag")
4. **Release title:** e.g. `Week 11 deck assets`
5. Under **Assets**, click **Attach files by dragging & dropping**
6. Add your file. Rename it to **`whatsapp-chat-screenrecord.mov`** (or upload and rename in the UI if needed).
   - Your file: `ScreenRecording_03-13-2026 13-36-15_1.mov` from Downloads
7. Publish the release.

After that, the public deck will use this video on slide 4:  
https://lildeebs.github.io/chronoai-internal/week-11-reporting/week-11-reporting.html
